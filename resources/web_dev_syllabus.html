FAQs
Can a complete beginner join this bootcamp? +
Absolutely! The course is designed for all levels. The youngest student was 11, the oldest 86. Some didn't even know what a website was when they started. All you need is perseverance and willingness to learn.
How long will this take to complete? +
In-person it runs over 12 weeks. Online students have completed it in as little as 3 weeks working full-time, or longer part-time. It's entirely at your own pace on Udemy.
Is the Udemy course the same as in-person? +
Yes! The online Udemy bootcamp has the exact same curriculum as the in-person bootcamp. The in-person version only runs once per year and is waitlist-based — Udemy is available anytime.
Where should I take the course — Udemy or appbrewery.com? +
Since 2019, all courses moved to Udemy. Get it from there.
